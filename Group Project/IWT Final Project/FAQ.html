Simply click Sign Up and provide necessary details and click register. You will receive a confirmation via e-mail and click verification link to access as a registered member.

Once you create an account and become a registered member, immediately you can start posting advertisements. Go to Post Your Ad and select the type of post and select property type and fill the designated form with necessary details and content. That’s it.

Samarasingha Land sale.lk has a designated division for quality assurance and content management. It will take from 1 hour to 24 hours to appear your advertisement and if you have any concern you can always shoot us an e-mail to support@Samarasinghalandsale.lk.

You can upload minimum 2 and up to 10 pictures per advertisement. The pictures can be JPG, GIF or TIFF format. We recommend keeping the picture size to less than 1 MB, however larger pictures will be auto re-sized when you upload. If the image upload is taking too long then it could be due to the size of the pictures and we recommend uploading pictures one by one in such situations.

You can post advertisements for properties located anywhere in Sri Lanka.

You can contact us via support@Samarasinghalandsale.lk and if you need any assistance by our technical assistance agent please mention your contact number and our team will contact you as soon as possible.

Head to the CeylonProperty.lk sign-in page and click the “Forgot Password” link. Click “Send verification” and verify it by clicking the link which will be sent to your e-mail.

You can login to your account and click on the My Ads and select the advertisement you want to edit and then click on the EDIT button to do the changes accordingly. We maintain high standard of quality assurance and even when you edit your advertisement you have to wait till the approval of our quality assurance and content management team.
